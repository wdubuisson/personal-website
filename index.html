A little about me

Currently based in Richmond, Virginia, my journey into tech started with a degree in Psychology. I have always been fascinated by the human mind and behavior; and these passions translated into a deep interest in how people interact with technology and systems. I believe that access to technology in this modern era is a human right, and it is the responsibility of web designers everywhere to ensure accessible design.

It is in the best interest of our society as a whole, business and consumers alike, that interfaces provide an optimal user experience. In 2018 I completed my Professional Certification in User Experience (UX) Design. I am now able to proficiently merge my web development skills with this user experience design knowledge to create interfaces that are elegant, functional, and usable.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, easy to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designer's PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
